About me

Hi! I'm Reedik, Head of Services Management Department at Estonian Transport Administration. By education I am an architect (a real one, not a system architect) and have actually practiced as one too during my studies and for a short period of time right after graduation.

I have also worked as a service manager (parcel machines, courier services) and as a head of a team that managed financial and data services for Estonian Post. After a three-year run as the CEO of one of the biggest Estonian architecture offices (KOKO architects) I decided to return to financial services, this time around within a bank - Luminor. As of July 2022, I am running the Services Management Department within Estonian Transport Administration and it has been a blast: fast paced dive into the world of land, air and sea transport - all in the demanding sauce of public services.

In my free time I like to keep myself busy with things I believe will help me stave off boredom - like studying HTML/CSS/JavaScript.
